About Gworkx Digital

Hey, I'm the guy behind Gworkx Digital.

I didn't start in marketing or websites. I spent years as a carpenter on real residential job sites in Queensland, Australia — framing houses, laying foundations, installing kitchens and bathrooms, doing siding, roofing (mostly corrugated iron on Queenslanders), tiling, drywall, painting, and pretty much everything else except plumbing and electrical.

I know what it's like to bust your ass from sunup to sundown, deal with tough clients, tight schedules, and the constant pressure of keeping the crew busy and the bills paid. I've been in the trenches, just like you.

That's why I get it when small New England contractors tell me their phone only rings from referrals, their Facebook page does nothing, and their crappy DIY website (or lack of one) is quietly costing them jobs.

I saw too many good tradesmen getting skipped over because homeowners couldn't find them online. So I took the skills I have and started building clean, straightforward websites specifically for 1–5 man crews like yours.

At Gworkx Digital, I don't do fancy marketing speak or monthly retainers. I build professional websites you fully own and hand it all over with one flat fee — no ongoing bullshit.

I'm not here to sell you dreams or "scale your business." I'm here to give hardworking New England contractors a solid online foundation that makes sense and actually works.

If you're tired of watching jobs go to the next guy with a better-looking online presence, and you're ready for something real and straightforward, let's talk.

Ready to get your shit sorted? Reach out.
